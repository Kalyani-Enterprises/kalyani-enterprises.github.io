About Us

Established in 1996, Kalyani Enterprises has anchored the industrial supply chain for over three decades as a premier Authorized Distributor of genuine HCH Bearings. We do not just supply parts; we secure production lines. By combining HCH's world-class automated manufacturing precision with our robust, centrally located inventory framework in Delhi, we provide Indian OEMs and enterprise manufacturers with an uncompromised, zero-disruption component lifecycle.

Our Mission

To eliminate mechanical failure and operational downtime across Indian industries by providing instant access to factory-certified, high-precision HCH rolling elements.

Our Vision

To be recognized as India's most trusted and technically proficient industrial bearing partner, setting the absolute benchmark for inventory depth, batch traceability, and procurement integrity.
